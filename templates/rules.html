How's
It
Played

Rule 1: Guess a five-letter word by entering your guess into the game interface.


Rule 2: After each guess, the game will display the feedback colors to help you narrow down the correct word.


Rule 3: You have a maximum of six attempts to guess the word correctly.


Rule 4: Use the feedback from each guess to deduce the correct letters and their positions in the word.


Rule 5: Eliminate letters that are not in the correct positions or not in the word at all based on the feedback.


Rule 6: he game is won when you guess the correct word within the given number of attempts.
